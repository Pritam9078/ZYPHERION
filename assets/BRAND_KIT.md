# Zypherion Brand Kit

## Logo Concept
- **Symbol**: A stylized "Z" intersected by a link or hexagonal mesh (representing the blockchain).
- **Core Theme**: Cyberpunk, Neon-noir, High-tech.

## Colors
- **Deep Space Blue**: #0a0b1e (Primary Background)
- **Neon Cyan**: #00f2fe (Action / Primary Accent)
- **Neon Purple**: #9d50bb (Secondary Accent)
- **Electric Blue**: #00d2ff (Glow Effects)

## Typography
- **Headings**: Orbitron or Inter (Bold) - Futurist feel.
- **Body**: Inter - Clean and readable.

## Design Elements
- **Glassmorphism**: 10px blur, 5% white opacity.
- **Neon Glows**: box-shadow: 0 0 15px [color].
- **Animations**: Subtle pulse and slide-ins.
